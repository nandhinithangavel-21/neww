Our Story

Born from a love of beautiful fabrics and timeless designs, ZaraBloom celebrates the modern Indian woman.

Who We Are

ZaraBloom was founded in 2019 with a singular vision: to make premium women's fashion accessible to every Indian woman. From the bustling streets of Bangalore to doorsteps across India, we've grown from a small boutique into a beloved online destination.

Our Philosophy

We believe fashion is deeply personal — an expression of who you are. Every piece in our collection is handpicked for quality, comfort, and style. We work directly with artisans and manufacturers across India to bring you exclusive designs that you won't find anywhere else.

Our Promise

  • Genuine quality — only the finest fabrics
  • Fair pricing — direct from makers to you
  • Inclusive sizing — S through XXL
  • Hassle-free returns — 15-day policy
  • Secure payments — 100% encrypted

Sustainability

We're committed to responsible fashion. Our packaging is 90% recyclable, and we partner with certified ethical manufacturers. Fashion that looks good and does good.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly — name, email, address, payment details — only for fulfilling your orders and improving your experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to process orders, send shipping updates, and provide customer support. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

All payment data is processed through PCI-DSS compliant gateways. We use SSL encryption across all our pages.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for cart functionality and analytics cookies to improve our store. You may disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

Payments

We accept UPI, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, Credit/Debit cards, and Net Banking. All transactions are in Indian Rupees (INR). We do not offer Cash on Delivery.
